About this website

I created a website dedicated to Charli XCX that celebrates her sound, visuals, and chaotic-pop energy through bold interaction and playful design. It’s a space where fandom meets experimentation, using dynamic layouts and immersive details to reflect her ever-evolving aesthetic while letting me explore the intersection of music, culture, and code. This project was coded by Natalie Kiel and is open sourced.

About me

I’m 30, based in Glasgow, Scotland, and work in interaction design while teaching myself full-stack coding. I’m driven by curiosity, building things that feel as good as they function, and constantly pushing my skills beyond design into development. When I’m not thinking about interfaces and code, I’m probably obsessing over Charli XCX—because great pop and great design both know how to hit hard and stay memorable.
